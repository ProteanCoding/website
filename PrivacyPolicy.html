• Privacy policy for Prototypes

Confidentiality is of great importance. A Privacy Policy is necessary even for applications which will not be collecting any user information.

This Privacy Policy applies when you try one of the Prototype applications deployed by Protean Productions.

These Prototypes are neither presented as games nor services, but as simple applications meant to showcase capabilities.

If any internet connection is required, it is only to query data that is anonymously used by the Prototype.

These Prototypes will not be transmitting, nor even collecting, any personal information.
